Summary

Inception is a 2010 science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The film revolves around a professional thief, Dom Cobb, who steals secrets from within the subconscious. Cobb is offered a chance to have his past crimes erased in exchange for planting an idea in someone's mind through a process known as "inception." However, the mission becomes increasingly dangerous as Cobb faces challenges from within his own mind.
